FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about the Honey Infused Lip Oil Tinted Trio.

Our Watermelon Sugar, Strawberry Sorbet, and Mango Passion Punch lip oils will be shipped in their individual packaging, not together in a single box.

Composed of a unique, intensely hydrating blend of Mirsalehi Honey and our Bee Garden Oils, with added Jojoba oil and Meadowfoam seed oil for even more hydration and longer wear, simply glide our non-sticky, shine formula onto bare lips to unveil the smoothest, glossiest, most irresistible lips.

While we wouldn't recommend making our lip oils your main course, we've infused them with scents so delicious, that you might be tempted to take a nibble. Each of our lippies has a specific scent that offers a delightful scent experience. Indulge in Mango Passion Punch, Strawberry Sorbet, and Watermelon Sugar, and let your lips feel the fruity freshness!
